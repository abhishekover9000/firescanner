Solve Wildfires with an Automated Prevention and Response System

Solar powered IOTs placed on towers with a large peripheral over forests can cleanly constantly scan for heat anomalies (aka brush fires) and immediately contacts fire departments.

Step 1 thermal vision IOTs are installed
Step 2 brushfire is detected (beyond campfire or cookout)
Step 3 IOT alerts nearby firestations through our cloud
Step 4 Fire engines react quickly and contain brushfire before it spirals out of control

Technology

IOT Unit

This solar powered unit will be equipped with thermal sight, weatherproof casing, and 4G connectivity.

Cloud server

The cloud server will handle all alerts processed by individual units and route to local fire departments that can quickly take action.

Progressive Webapp

This app displays all alerts to the fire departments and can be viewed on tablets at the station or individual mobile devices for fire engines on the go.
